Design Beautiful Ads

Posting your Poster Advertisement on the noticeboard is easy. You first need to upload the artwork of your AD either as an image or PDF.

You would then go ahead to select the size, paper type, location and duration of the AD for each location. The price of posting the AD in each location would be displayed.

Book and Pay

Once all options have been selected to satisfaction, your free to check out and pay for your advertisement online, Through Mpesa, Paypal or Visa/Master Card. A receipt would be sent to your email and the transaction would be saved in your account.

Terms Of Service

Printed and Posted

Upon Successful payment, Your AD will be vetted to ensure it does not violet our terms of service. Thereafter it would be printed at a central location then delivered and posted in all the locations you had selected by the next day.
